BRYAN In 2014, Shoshana Zuboff coins the term ‘surveillance capitalism’. This is when data is gathered about people in secret, or with there nominal consent. This data, or metadata about that data, is sold; or products are marketed using that data. Results: constant commercial surveillance, you think your phone is reading your mind, and what else?
BRYAN Privacy violations have many known harms. Most malware comes from ads. More sinister are phishing attacks that lead to data breaches or doxing. Hey give us your email? JUNIOR When you visit a website, that website may have other content that is being loaded from other websites, such as advertising and ad-tracking software. Pi-hole blocks the requests for those websites. Pi-hole acts as a DNS server, that is, it associates IP addresses with name addresses.

What does PI-hole do?

PI-hole blocks ad querIes.

PI-hole Is a DNS server.

JUNIOR This is how Pi-Hole works. Your browser makes a request for a website. That request is sent to Pi-Hole. Pi-Hole as a DNS server has a list of pre-loaded known advertising URLs. It checks if the website you requested is also requesting data to be sent from the black-list of URLs. If so, it blocks that content and only serves what’s on the website.
